LOCATION

Holy Family Convent School is situated in Gohana , Panipat Road 3 km ahead of Panipat Chungi . It is quite adjacent to High way No.71 A. The construction of School building is aesthetically designed. There are two separate buildings meant for Kinder Garten and school section. Class rooms are spacious and airy, uninterrupted power back up along with latest amenities. We believe that real education does not take place only with in the class rooms but outside the class rooms as well. Campus provides every opportunity and facilities for every student to develop his/ her personality to the maximum potential. Students are encouraged to participate in extracurricular and co curricular activities.
