PATRON OF THE INSTITUTION - OUR INSPIRATION

Great edifices are built on strong foundation with unshaken corner stones. Lord Jesus Christ, Beloved Mother Virgin Mary and Compassionate foster father St. Joseph – The Holy Family - is our inspiration. The righteousness of Joseph and the humanity of Mary shined brightly through the light “that enlighten all men” The deep faith and surrendering attitude of Mary and Joseph made their earthly home Heaven. The sisters of Holy Family are called by God to witness familial love and communion and to make the families of the world holy after the model of the Holy Family of Nazareth.
