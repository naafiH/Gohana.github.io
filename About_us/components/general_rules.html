School fee

School fees are fixed by the governing body and are subject to revision if found necessary. Fees are collected in the bank of Baroda from 5th to 15th of April , July , October and January. Fee details are available in the school office. Admission in pre primary classes starts in the month of January. There is no regular registration for the other classes and admission to such classes is made only when vacancies occur.
